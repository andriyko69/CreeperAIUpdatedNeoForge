## About This Port

This version brings **Creeper AI Updated** to **NeoForge 1.21.1**, keeping the original gameplay concept while updating the codebase and project structure for modern Minecraft modding.

The goal of this port is to:

- Maintain compatibility with NeoForge 1.21.1
- Preserve the original behavior and design
- Keep the project open-source under GPL-3.0
- Provide continued usability for modern modpacks

---

## Features

Inspired by Epic Siege Mod, Creeper AI Updated gives creepers the ability to **bomb their way to you**, making hiding inside your base far less safe.

Creepers gain enhanced abilities such as:

- Breaking through walls to reach targets
- Spawning as powered (charged) creepers
- Leaping at targets like spiders
- Causing fire explosions
- Extended follow/awareness range

---

## Configuration Options

This mod allows you to configure:

- Probability of creepers spawning powered
- Follow / awareness range
- Distance at which creepers decide to breach walls
- Whether creepers can break walls
- Creeper leap ability
- Fire-causing explosions
- Minimum and maximum Y-level for creepers spawning with wall-breaching ability
